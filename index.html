How To Prepare For Home Games On The Blue

By Stuart Tregenza

  • Tags
  • Nested
  • Media

The first and most important way to prepare ones self for a Boise State home game is to get a ticket. To do this students must visit broncosports.com then follow a link at the top of the page to ticketmaster.com. Here, students will plug in the PROMO code "STUDENT" and booom, theres a free ticket to the BSU home game.

Terms & Conditions: Only full-time BSU students are eligible for free student tickets, part-time students get discounted tickets. While supplies last, ticket sales are based off of a first come, first serve basis.

Home Team Spirit

Many games throughout the season have themes like "White-Out" or "Black-Out" which are awesome but as long as fans wear BSU gear or blue and orange they'll fit in with the crowd.
