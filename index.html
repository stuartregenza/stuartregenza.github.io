How To Prepare For Home Games On The Blue

By Stuart Tregenza

The first and most important way to prepare ones self for a Boise State home game is to get a ticket. To do this students must visit broncosports.com then follow a link at the top of the page to ticketmaster.com. Here, students will plug in the PROMO code "STUDENT" and booom, theres a free ticket to the BSU home game.

Terms & Conditions: Only full-time BSU students are eligible for free studnet tickets, part-time students get discounted tickets. While supplies last, ticket sales are based off of a first come, first serve basis.
-This is the BSU ticketmaster site @ https://am.ticketmaster. com/boisestateathl/

Home Team Spirit
